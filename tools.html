Frequently Asked Questions

What is TAC in printing?

Total Area Coverage (TAC) is the total percentage of CMYK ink used in a specific print area. Keeping this within limits prevents ink from smudging, pooling, and failing to dry on the high-speed printing press.

What is a dieline?

A dieline is a flat vector template that shows the precise cut lines, fold lines, and glue flaps required for packaging production, die-cutting, and 3D box folding.

Why is barcode safe zone important?

A barcode safe zone ensures that optical and laser scanners can read the barcode correctly without interference from surrounding packaging graphics, thereby preventing massive retail product scanning rejections.
