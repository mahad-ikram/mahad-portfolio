Frequently Asked Questions

What does a Prepress Executive do for FMCG packaging?

A prepress executive ensures that digital packaging designs are technically flawless before mass production. This includes managing dielines, color separations, bleeds, and trapping for high-speed offset printing lines, ensuring the final physical product matches the creative vision.

Do you offer freelance communication design services globally?

Yes. While my technical print operations are based in Karachi, Pakistan, I work with clients worldwide via platforms like Fiverr to deliver 360-degree brand campaigns, creative strategy, and premium structural packaging designs tailored to global markets.

What is the benefit of hiring a designer with a prepress background?

Hiring a creative lead with prepress expertise eliminates costly printing errors. It ensures that the creative visuals designed on-screen are mathematically and technically viable for actual production, saving brands significant time and material costs during the manufacturing phase.
