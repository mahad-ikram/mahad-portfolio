Straight Tuck End Box (STE) Dieline

The global standard for premium retail packaging. Both closure flaps tuck into the rear of the box, providing a clean front face.

Reverse Tuck End Box (RTE) Dieline

The most common and cost-effective carton. The top flap tucks to the rear, while the bottom flap tucks to the front. Because the flaps alternate, RTE boxes nest tightly together on a printing sheet, reducing paperboard waste and saving costs.

Gable Top Box (Milk Carton)

A complex structural design utilizing 45-degree folding gussets and an interlocking top seal. Highly popular for FMCG food, beverage, and premium gift boxes.

Packaging Sleeve Template

A simple open-ended tube structure perfect for sliding over coffee cups, custom soap boxes, or premium food trays. Requires only a single glue flap.
